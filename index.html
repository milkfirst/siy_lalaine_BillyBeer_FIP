About Us


Since 2023, Billy Beer has been inspiring beer enthusiasts to enjoy raise a glass to a good time and enjoy life's simple pleasures. Whether it's sipping by the lake on a warm summer day, or relaxing après-ski with friends, our refreshing and flavorful beers are the perfect accompaniment to your outdoor adventures. At Billy Beer, we believe that the best moments in life are spent outside, with a refreshing bucket of ice-cold Billy Beers.



OUR STORY

Billy is more than just a beer; it's a way of life. Its confident and unassuming style, Billy Beer is a brand that embodies the spirit of living life to the fullest and enjoying every moment.
